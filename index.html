Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out.

Why is your pricing higher than regular web designers? +
We don't sell websites — we build business systems. Our platforms are designed to solve real business problems, automate operations, increase trust, and support growth. This approach delivers long-term value, not just visuals.
What determines the final cost of a project? +
The cost depends on:
  • The problem the platform is solving
  • Number of features and integrations
  • Level of automation required
  • Security, performance, and scalability needs
After a brief consultation, we recommend the most suitable system, not the most expensive one.
Do you use templates? +
No. Every project is custom-designed and built from scratch to match your brand, business goals, and users. This ensures better performance, uniqueness, and scalability.
How does payment work for large projects? +
For mid-to-large projects, we use a milestone-based payment structure:
  • 40% upfront – project kickoff
  • 40% mid-development – after key milestones
  • 20% on delivery – after final approval
This protects both parties and ensures transparency.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work? +
We offer a milestone guarantee. If a project milestone is not delivered as agreed, we revise it at no extra cost — or refund the affected portion. Your satisfaction and trust matter to us.
How long does it take to complete a project? +
Delivery time depends on the project scope:
  • Starter System: 7–10 days
  • Growth System: 3–5 weeks
  • Enterprise System: 2–4 months
Clear timelines are provided before work begins.
Will my website work on mobile devices? +
Yes. All our systems are mobile-first and fully responsive, ensuring smooth performance on phones, tablets, and desktops.
Do you provide support after launch? +
Yes. Every plan includes post-launch support. Enterprise clients receive priority support and long-term maintenance options.
Can you integrate payments, APIs, or third-party tools? +
Absolutely. We integrate:
  • Paystack, Flutterwave & other gateways
  • APIs and third-party services
  • Automation tools and analytics
Enterprise systems can include custom or AI-powered integrations.
Is ₦10,000,000 really worth it? +
For businesses that need scalability, security, automation, and performance, yes. You're not paying for a website — you're investing in a digital asset that supports revenue and growth.
Who are these services best for? +
Our systems are ideal for:
  • Startups & SMEs ready to scale
  • Agencies & service companies
  • Corporations & tech-driven businesses
If your business relies on customers, data, or automation, this is for you.
How do we get started? +
Simple:
  1. Book a consultation
  2. We identify the problem to solve
  3. We recommend the right system
  4. Project kickoff after payment
